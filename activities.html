Artwork Gallery

Here is a showcase of some artwork of mine that I would like to highlight. I chose these three works because they demonstrate a combination of physical (pencil and paper sketching) as well as digital enhancements and rending in software such as Adobe Photoshop.

Descriptions

“If Only I Could Fly”
This image depicts a silhouette of an individual attempting to catapult from a tree tied to a stump, the scene and its reflection together forming the shape of an aircraft.
“Above and Beyond”
This image is a submission of mine for a T-Shirt design contest by my employer, Octo Consulting Group. It includes the company logo, with a person standing on hand-drawn mountains and octagon-shaped clouds.
“Autumn Street”
This image is an illustration of a suburban street during the season of autumn, with leaves on the ground and a strong, dusty shade of twilight over the landscape produced by a setting sun.
